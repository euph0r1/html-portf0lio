MY personel top 3 films

Fantastic Mr fox

Yes it may be surprising but this Wes Anderson masterpiece is basically a cinema breaththrough in it of itself.
from the ironic sense of humor to the emotional climax,the movie is sheer perfection.
i mean, how could someone turn a children's book into such a philosophical and humorous story is beyond me ,and im not even mentioning the style here, because i am pretty sure you have already heard about Wes's cinematic style by now

Inglorious Basterds

Not listing a Quentin tarantino movie here would simply be a crime, for he is most probably my favorite film-maker of all time,nonetheless, this piece of art is what reminds me why i love cinema in the first place
From the uneasy tension of the opening scene to the masterful chaos of the final act , i simply worship this movie , and i beleive everyone should too.

Memento

The best christopher nolan movie in my opnion, yet the most underrrated.
